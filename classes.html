About

Mysore Classes

The Self-Paced/Mysore Practice is an individualized approach to yoga where each student is taught a sequence of yoga postures, paced according to their own needs and physical condition. We generally start with shorter practices of about 30 minutes, and then increase the duration slowly, according to the student’s capacity.

Led Classes

The traditional led primary series (foundational practice) classes are teacher guided by using a counting method in Sanskrit which is aligned with each movement and breath to allow the students to understand the rhythm and the tempo of the Vinyasa system.

What to Bring

  • Please bring your own yoga mat, which can be stored at the studio. For the trial week we’ll lend you a mat.
  • Dress in comfy, light and stretchy cloths.
  • It is best to bring a small towel for perspiration.

What to Consider

  • You can turn up to class anytime during studio opening hours.
  • A beginner class takes approx. 30 – 60 min., while a more advanced class might take 90 – 120 min.
  • Please mention any injuries, illnesses or pregnancy to your teacher.
  • Please let your teacher know If you prefer to not have hands-on adjustments to your yoga poses.
  • Yoga is best practiced on an empty stomach, so avoid eating a large meal for at least 3 hours before class.
  • Please shower before class and refrain from wearing fragrances as to respect other practitioner's concentration.
