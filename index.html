Our Intention

Share Traditional Yoga

Mysore + Led Classes

Our philosophy is to honor authentic Ashtanga Yoga in the tradition of the founder, Sri. K. Pattabhi Jois by teaching mysore style classes and guided led classes. By following a specific series of postures and a technique called vinyasa, which means the linking of the breath and movement together, you’ll balance your body, mind and spirit.

Invite Self-Discovery

Returning to your essence

We are here to honor and empower your journey toward personal growth and well-being. This authentic yoga system gives us an opportunity to find inner peace and connect to our true self. Returning to our inherent wholeness brings us balance and harmony not only with ourselves but also with nature’s cycles and rhythms.

Connect To Nature

Our greatest teacher

Mother nature is our greatest teacher and inspiration. We encourage connecting to nature by holding outdoor classes and nature walks. Our yoga community gets involved with environmental projects to honor nature’s preservation. Also, Ashtanga yoga’s recommended vegetarian diet aids in nature’s sustainability.

Join our community

The proceeds of our classes go partly to social and environmental projects, whereby we get involved in a variety of ways.

At times we hold yoga classes in nature.

We organize meetups for nature walks, picnics, afternoon tea among others to connect with one another.
